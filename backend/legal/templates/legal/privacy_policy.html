Mr. May Who LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates whodinees.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address when you create an order
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store full credit card numbers)
  • Pet Photos: Images you upload to generate 3D models
  • Order Details: Shipping address, order history, preferences

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, interactions (via Google Analytics)
  • Session Recordings: User interactions on key pages (via Hotjar) to improve user experience
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, cookies
  • Error Logs: Crash reports and errors (via Sentry) to maintain service quality

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Generate 3D models from your pet photos using AI
  • Communicate order status, updates, and customer support
  • Process payments securely through Stripe
  • Send transactional emails (order confirmations, invoices, shipping updates)
  • Send marketing emails (abandoned cart reminders, review requests) with opt-out options
  • Improve our website and services through analytics
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only with trusted third-party services:

3.1 Service Providers

  • Meshy AI: Processes pet photos to generate 3D models
  • Shapeways: 3D printing fulfillment partner
  • Stripe: Payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • SendGrid: Email delivery service
  • Heroku: Cloud hosting platform
  • Cloudflare: Content delivery and security
  • Google Analytics: Website analytics
  • Hotjar: Session recordings and heatmaps
  • Sentry: Error monitoring

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

3.3 Business Transfers

If we are acquired or merge with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Retention

  • Order Data: Retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Pet Photos: Retained for 90 days after order completion, then deleted
  • 3D Models: Retained for 1 year to enable reorders
  • Marketing Data: Retained until you opt out or request deletion

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails via the link in each email
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object: Object to processing for marketing purposes

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@whodinees.com

6. Cookies and Tracking

6.1 Essential Cookies

Required for the website to function (session management, security)

6.2 Analytics Cookies

Google Analytics and Hotjar track usage to improve our service. You can opt out:

  • Google Analytics: Browser Add-on
  • Hotjar: Do Not Track

6.3 Advertising Cookies

We do not currently use advertising cookies or retargeting pixels.

7. Data Security

We protect your information using:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure cloud infrastructure (Heroku, Cloudflare)
  • PCI-DSS compliant payment processing (Stripe)
  • Rate limiting and abuse detection
  • Regular security audits and monitoring (Sentry)
  • Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, contact us immediately at hello@whodinees.com

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States. We ensure adequate safeguards are in place to protect your data in compliance with applicable laws.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (not applicable)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

To exercise these rights, email us at hello@whodinees.com

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal basis for processing: Contract performance, legitimate interests, consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to restrict or object to processing

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
