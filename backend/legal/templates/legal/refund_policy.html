At Whodinees, we want you to be completely satisfied with your custom 3D printed pet figurine. This Refund Policy explains our refund and return process.

1. Overview

Due to the custom nature of our products, refund eligibility depends on the stage of your order:

  • Before model approval: Full refund available (minus $25 deposit)
  • After model approval: No refunds (production has begun)
  • Defective products: Full refund or replacement
  • Shipping damage: Replacement at no cost

2. The $25 Deposit

2.1 Non-Refundable

The $25 deposit is non-refundable once model generation begins. This deposit covers:

  • AI processing fees (Meshy AI)
  • Server and compute costs
  • Administrative overhead

2.2 Exceptions

The deposit may be refunded if:

  • We are unable to generate a model from your photo (technical failure)
  • We cancel your order due to service unavailability
  • There is a billing error

3. Refund Before Model Approval

3.1 Full Refund Eligibility

If you are unhappy with the generated 3D model, you may reject it and receive a full refund (minus the $25 deposit) before approving the model.

3.2 Process

  1. Review your generated 3D model in the model viewer
  2. If unsatisfied, click "Request Changes" or contact us at hello@whodinees.com
  3. We will work with you to improve the model (up to 3 revision attempts)
  4. If you are still unsatisfied after revisions, we will issue a refund (minus deposit)

3.3 Timeline

You have 7 days to review and approve or reject your model. If you do not respond within 7 days, the model will be automatically approved and production will begin.

4. No Refunds After Model Approval

4.1 Production Has Begun

Once you approve the 3D model and complete payment, we immediately send your order to our production partner (Shapeways). At this point:

  • Your custom figurine is being manufactured
  • Materials have been allocated
  • Production costs are incurred

No refunds are available after model approval and full payment.

4.2 Exceptions

Refunds after approval are only available for:

  • Defective products (see Section 5)
  • Shipping damage (see Section 6)
  • Production failure (we are unable to complete your order)

5. Defective Product Refunds

5.1 What Qualifies as Defective?

  • Significant structural defects (cracks, breaks, missing parts)
  • Material defects (discoloration, impurities, wrong material)
  • Production errors (model does not match approved design)
  • Quality issues that make the figurine unusable or significantly flawed

5.2 What Is NOT Defective?

  • Minor color variations (inherent in 3D printing)
  • Small surface imperfections or texture variations
  • Differences between the digital model and physical product (normal for 3D printing)
  • Personal dissatisfaction with the appearance (you approved the model)

5.3 Defect Claim Process

  1. Contact us at hello@whodinees.com within 14 days of delivery
  2. Provide photos clearly showing the defect
  3. Describe the issue in detail
  4. We will review your claim within 3 business days
  5. If approved, we will offer a replacement or full refund (your choice)

6. Shipping Damage

6.1 Damaged in Transit

If your figurine arrives damaged due to shipping:

  1. Take photos of the damaged product and packaging
  2. Contact us at hello@whodinees.com within 7 days of delivery
  3. We will file a claim with the shipping carrier
  4. We will send a replacement at no cost to you

6.2 Lost Packages

  • If tracking shows your package as delivered but you did not receive it, contact us immediately
  • We will investigate with the carrier
  • If the package is confirmed lost, we will send a replacement at no cost

7. Refund Processing

7.1 Timeline

  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of approval
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method via Stripe
  • It may take an additional 5-10 business days for your bank to process the refund

7.2 Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer a partial refund as compensation for minor issues that do not warrant a full refund or replacement.

8. Returns

8.1 No Returns on Custom Products

Because each figurine is custom-made for you, we do not accept returns for change of mind, buyer's remorse, or aesthetic dissatisfaction after approval.

8.2 Defect Returns

If you choose a refund instead of a replacement for a defective product:

  • We will provide a prepaid return shipping label
  • You must return the defective product within 14 days
  • Refund will be issued upon receipt of the returned item

9. Special Circumstances

9.1 Duplicate Orders

If you accidentally place multiple orders for the same item, contact us immediately at hello@whodinees.com. We may be able to cancel the duplicate order before production begins.

9.2 Wrong Address

  • If you provide an incorrect shipping address, we are not responsible for delivery failures
  • Contact us as soon as possible to update your address before shipment
  • Reshipping to a corrected address will incur additional shipping fees

9.3 Customs Fees (International Orders)

  • International customers are responsible for customs duties and import taxes
  • We do not refund orders refused due to customs fees
  • Check your country's import regulations before ordering

10. Refund Exceptions

We reserve the right to deny refund requests in cases of:

  • Fraudulent claims or abuse of the refund policy
  • Damage caused by customer misuse or neglect
  • Requests made outside the specified timeframes
  • Violations of our Terms of Service

11. Goodwill Gestures

Even in cases where a refund is not technically required, we may offer:

  • Discount codes for future orders
  • Free reprint or replacement
  • Partial refunds as a goodwill gesture

These are discretionary and do not create an obligation for future similar situations.

We stand behind our products and are committed to your satisfaction. If you have any concerns, please reach out to us at hello@whodinees.com
